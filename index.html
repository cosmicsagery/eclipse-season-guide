What Eclipse Season Is Actually Doing to You

Every eclipse season, the same thing happens. People feel off. They feel exhausted, anxious, emotional, or physically unwell in ways they can’t explain. They brace for it. They white-knuckle through it. Some have had eclipses rearrange their entire lives without warning.

And almost nobody understands why.

So let’s start here, with what’s actually happening — because once you understand the mechanics, everything changes.

Your Crown Chakra Opens

During an eclipse window, the crown chakra — the energy center at the top of your head — spins faster than usual. Much faster. This is the defining energetic feature of any eclipse season.

When the crown spins this fast, your soul can access your consciousness in ways it normally can’t. The filters and distortions that usually sit between your soul and your lived experience — the ones shaped by society’s rules, by the systems you were raised in, by beliefs you may not even know you carry — get thinner. In some cases, nearly gone.

Your soul, in that opening, starts working. Fast. Think of it like pure code coming in and fixing all the bugs at once. It’s rewriting old programming, clearing out what no longer serves, and laying new groundwork for the life that’s trying to come through you.

This is what I call soul liberation. It’s not a crisis. It’s not a punishment. It’s not the universe testing you. It is a window where your soul can do what it’s been waiting to do.

So Why Does It Feel So Hard?

Because your human consciousness — your body, your nervous system, your survival wiring — has its own response to the speed of change. And that response is completely natural.

Your soul is limitless and infinite. Human consciousness is structured, formed, built around rules and the familiar. When the soul starts editing that fast, the human part of you goes: too much, too soon, too fast.

And it reaches for the brakes.

The Three Layers of Natural Resistance

As your consciousness expands — whether through an eclipse, through spiritual development, through any genuine growth — it will resist. Your consciousness will resist the experience of going from what it knows to what it does not know.

Why? Because what you know, on the scale of safe versus unsafe, is always perceived as safe. Always. It doesn’t matter if you like it. It doesn’t matter if it’s actually good for you. What is known feels safer than what is unknown. And what is unknown — well, we don’t know it. So we have no way to know if it’s safe. So it must be unsafe.

Your consciousness’s job is survival. It is also heavily influenced by the programs of the systems you were raised in — systems that have used fear to maintain control of your decisions and actions. So when expansion happens, your consciousness reaches into those programs for reasons to stop.

This resistance shows up in three layers. They generally come in this order, though your consciousness can get creative and throw them at you simultaneously or in whatever combination it thinks will work best.

Layer 1: Laziness

This is the most common first response. It sounds like: I don’t have time. I’m too busy. I’m too tired. I’ll do it later. It’s not that big a deal. I already did it once this week — that’s enough.

During eclipse season, this shows up as needing excessive naps, sleeping in, inability to focus, scrolling endlessly without purpose. Your consciousness is pulling the blankets over its head.

The gift in this layer: view the tiredness as your body conserving fuel for the fire. Don’t fight rest when it’s genuine. But check it — are you resting, or are you hiding?

Layer 2: Fear

When you push past the laziness — when you do it anyway — fear arrives. It says: Are you sure? And you think, maybe I’m not sure. I don’t know. That’s really scary.

Your consciousness will scan the database of your life experience and find whatever fear really gets to you. Whatever one works. It doesn’t have to be rational or related to what’s actually happening. It might bring up fears connected to your expansion — or it might pull something completely unrelated because it knows that’s the one that stops you.

During eclipse season, this layer feels like anxiety but isn’t anxiety. It’s intrusive thoughts: Are you really going to do that? How do you know you can afford that? Who do you think you are? These are past beliefs. They don’t align with who you are now.

When this comes up: get curious. Where did this fear come from? Is it mine or someone else’s? Who benefits from me being afraid of this? And write it down — don’t leave it in your head. Your mind will muddle it. Put it on paper where you can see it clearly.

Layer 3: Pain

This is the deepest and most convincing layer. If laziness and fear didn’t stop you, pain will try.

Headaches out of nowhere. Body aches. Illness that appears with no clear cause. A heaviness that makes you believe something is physically wrong. This is your consciousness creating a full shutdown response — its last attempt to stop the expansion.

During eclipse season, when the soul is editing so rapidly, this layer can feel very real and very physical. But the pain is not a sign something is wrong. It is a sign your consciousness is trying its last strategy to slow down what the soul is doing.

The Key

Your consciousness gives you three opportunities to turn back through these layers. Once you’ve moved through all three, you’re free and clear in this moment of expansion — until the next one. You will always experience these layers as you grow. But your ability to recognize them, to name them, to move through them? That gets faster every time.

The three layers of natural resistance are taught in full in Lesson 1 of The Conscious Psychic, found inside Awaken Academy.

What This Eclipse Season Is About

Now that you understand the mechanics — the crown opening, the soul liberating, the body resisting — let’s talk about this specific eclipse window. Because this one is rare, and it’s carrying something powerful with it.

The Window: February 17 – March 3

On February 17, 2026, a Ring of Fire solar eclipse opens a 14-day window. On March 3 — which is also 3-3, the number of karmic patterns — a Blood Moon lunar eclipse closes it.

February 17 is also the start of the Lunar New Year: the Year of the Fire Horse. The transition from the Year of the Snake is the eclipse window itself. The snake sheds. The horse arrives. But you don’t just jump on the horse. The 14 days are the preparation.

The Vision

You are standing inside a ring of fire on the ground. Everything inside the ring is bright. Everything outside is darkness. For 14 days, you dance in that ring — feeling your soul’s unique rhythm.

On March 3, a dark horse emerges at the edge of the ring of fire, moving through it. You leap onto the horse’s back, barebacked, and you ride.

Power and Precision

These are the two words for the Year of the Fire Horse. A horse has immense power. But its hooves are delicate, sensitive, precise on any terrain. Your soul has immense power too. The fire horse teaches that power is not bulldozing. Power is moving with precision.

The deeper theme is freedom of movement. Freedom of time. Freedom of location. Freedom of connection and decision. Being where you want, going where you want, doing what you want, connecting with who you want.

Fire horse energy is thriving in chaos without being exhausted by it. The storm can be around you — you don’t need to be in it. It doesn’t have to exhaust you. When the forces around you are giving you momentum to move forward and you’re not fighting it, it’s great.

So What Do You Do With This?

Now you know what eclipse season actually is. You know why the tiredness hits, why the anxiety-that-isn’t-anxiety shows up, why your body might be producing headaches or pain out of nowhere. You know it’s not a sign something is wrong — it’s a sign your soul is moving fast.

But knowing isn’t enough. Not during a window like this.

Because the three layers of resistance don’t stop just because you understand them. Tiredness will still try to pull you under the covers. Fear will still scan your database for the thought that gets to you most. Pain will still show up and try to convince you something is physically wrong.

The difference is this: there are two specific practices that keep your body in rhythm with the soul instead of fighting it. They are the reason some people white-knuckle through eclipse season while others come out the other side riding the horse.

The Two Daily Practices

If Part One explained what’s happening and why, Part Two is what you do about it.

There are two practices for this eclipse window. Do them every day from February 17 through March 3. They are what keep you in rhythm with your soul instead of bracing against it. They are what build the power and precision that arrive on 3-3, when you vault onto the horse.

Inside our Awaken Academy membership, we did the first practice together on a live group Zoom call on the first day of the eclipse window. The results were immediate. Members reported their feet going hot, shoulder tension releasing after weeks of holding, solar plexus shifts, energy surging through the body, and crown pressure that had felt like it wanted to be a headache dissolving into something entirely different.

You can do both of these alone, in your own space, on your own time. Here’s how.

Practice 1: Dance in Your Ring of Fire

Close your eyes. Let your body move however it needs to.

It doesn’t have to look like dancing. No one is watching — and that’s exactly the point. Eyes closed is best because you stop watching yourself, stop wondering if it looks good, and start feeling from the inside out. This is not about the outside. It’s about how it feels in your body.

Your soul is fire. Fire is always dancing, always moving. It is your willpower, your strength, your will to go on when how could you otherwise. When you close your eyes and move, you drop into your soul’s unique rhythm and cadence and flow.

With the crown so open right now, your soul is more accessible than it may have ever been. Dancing makes space for that connection on purpose. It keeps you in rhythm with what your soul is doing instead of bracing against it. This is the single most important thing you can do during this window.

Ways to Make It Your Own

  • Light candles in a circle on the floor and dance inside it
  • Use a fire pit or fire table as your ring of fire
  • Turn off the lights, line up candles, and get lost in the space
  • Play shamanic drumming or tuning forks
  • Fire gaze while you move
  • Dance with music — or in total silence
  • Sway gently if standing feels like too much right now
  • Chair dance at work with your headphones in when the agitation hits
  • Bring sensuality into it — on your own or with a partner
  • When you’re facing a block or conflict in your day, stay calm inside it — that’s dancing in the conflict too

You would never meet a horse for the first time and just jump on. You would prep. The 14-day eclipse window IS the prep. You build rhythm. You build flow. On March 3, when the dark horse emerges, you’re ready.

And here’s the thing: you don’t leave your ring of fire when the eclipse ends. You dance it into your body, into your life, such that it goes everywhere you go. You are both dancing in your fire and riding the horse at the same time.

Practice 2: Soul Chats in the Mirror

Look at yourself in the mirror and let your soul speak.

This can start as affirmations — but it’s meant to go far beyond that. It’s a conversation. With yourself, with your soul, with whatever is trying to come through. You are giving your soul permission to use your voice.

Maybe you talk about what you just felt after dancing. Maybe you talk about what feels hard right now. Maybe you talk yourself through the resistance: I know there’s resistance coming up. I know the tiredness, the fear, the pain. I understand that the human part of me is having a moment. I also understand that my soul is liberating itself, and that’s going to liberate my experience in life.

Messages trying to come through may come out of your mouth before your mind catches up. Understanding for what’s happening and why may just arrive, mid-sentence. That’s the soul talking. Let it.

Ramp it up by lighting a candle in front of the mirror. The fire adds connection and presence. The soul chats become permission — permission for your soul to be free.

Supporting Your Body Through the Window

The two practices above are the daily work. Everything below is what helps your body feel safe enough to let the soul do its work without the resistance winning.

Ground to the Center of the Earth

For as open as the crown chakra is, the root chakra must be equally open to balance it. The soul is infinite — it needs connection to the infinite below to balance the infinite above.

Use the Tree of Life meditation form: your roots go out wide and deep, your branches expand out and up. Within that structure, a funnel rises up through the root chakra to the sky and back down. A funnel descends through the crown to the center of Earth and back up. The two spiral around each other like DNA helix strands — they don’t connect at the funnel points, they spiral.

When the crown activity is intense, go as deep as possible. Sit at the center of the Earth with Mother Earth. Let her heartbeat — that pulsation — move through your body. It brings you into a steadiness. The warmth that comes is your nervous system fully regulating. Your body remembering it is safe.

The full Tree of Life meditation form is taught in Lesson 2 of the Awaken Academy curriculum.

Quick Root Chakra Tips

  • Don’t cross your legs, feet, knees, or any part of the body — this keeps the root chakra open
  • Bare feet on the ground whenever possible
  • Hug a tree
  • If the crown gets overwhelming, the first thing to check: are my legs crossed?

Let Your Physical Body Be Steady

The soul is moving rapidly. The physical body is not supposed to match that pace. What feels like urgency or antsiness — that’s what’s happening at the soul’s level, not a call to action for your body.

Find what brings your physical body into a steady state. This looks different for everyone:

  • Rest and absorption — laying down with a course, a book, a podcast
  • Walking, gentle movement, jogging
  • Nesting — building something, organizing, assembling, puttering
  • Anything that keeps you grounded in the moment so the antsiness doesn’t spiral into overdrive-then-crash

When you feel the urge to do 15 things at once — slow down. Communicate your needs. That is the new pattern.

Drink More Water Than You Think You Need

Your body is processing an extraordinary amount of energy during this window. Warm lemon water in the morning. Loaded water throughout the day — electrolytes, collagen, whatever nutrients you can consolidate into one drink. Keep a backup bottle close. You will go through it.

Talk to Your Body

When pain or discomfort shows up, speak to it. Not from a place of fighting it — from a place of understanding.

You are not arguing with your body. You are reminding it of evidence it already has: that you have survived every transformation. That safety is not the absence of change.

Now Make It Yours

You have the two practices. You know what to do every day between now and March 3. But here’s the thing about a 14-day window of soul liberation: it’s going to move things in you that you didn’t expect.

The dancing will unlock something. The mirror will show you something. A layer of resistance will hit and you’ll need somewhere to put what’s coming up — not just feel it, but see it, track it, understand what’s shifting.

That’s what Part Three is for.

The workbook gives you affirmations you can use in your soul chats — words for the moments when you’re standing in front of the mirror and nothing comes out yet. It gives you daily check-in prompts so you can track which layers of resistance are showing up and watch them change over 14 days. And it gives you the deeper questions — the ones that help you see what your soul is actually liberating, what pattern is being rewritten, and what you want to ride toward when the dark horse arrives on 3-3.

Without it, the practices still work. With it, you’ll be able to look back on March 3 and see exactly what moved, what broke open, and what you became.
